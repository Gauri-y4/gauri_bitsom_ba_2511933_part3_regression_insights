\# **Residual Analysis**



\## Predicted Sales and Residuals



Predicted sales were calculated using the final multiple regression model.



Residuals were calculated as:



Residual = Actual Sales − Predicted Sales



Positive residuals indicate that actual sales were higher than predicted.



Negative residuals indicate that actual sales were lower than predicted.

\## Business Interpretation



The residual analysis shows that although the regression model explains a large proportion of the variation in monthly sales (R² ≈ 0.81), some stores still differ substantially from the predicted values.



The model may under-predict sales for certain high-performing stores and over-predict sales for some lower-performing stores. This suggests that additional business factors not included in the dataset may influence store performance.



Overall, the model provides strong predictive performance but should be used alongside business knowledge rather than as the only decision-making tool.
